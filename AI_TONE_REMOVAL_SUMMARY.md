## Writing Principles Applied

✅ **Active Voice** - "We built this" not "This was built"
✅ **Concise Sentences** - Removed unnecessary phrases
✅ **Parallel Structure** - Similar ideas match grammatical form
✅ **Clear Purpose** - Each section answers one question
✅ **Natural Tone** - Conversational, not corporate
✅ **No Fluff** - Removed padding and filler
✅ **Emphasis Placement** - Important info at the end
✅ **Scannable Format** - Short paragraphs, clear headings

## What This Means

The documentation now reads like something written by an actual person, not generated by AI.

No more:
- Excessive emoji and special characters
- Lists of "verified" items with checkmarks
- Overly formal language mixed with casual
- Repetitive headings and subheadings

- Information organized for actual use
